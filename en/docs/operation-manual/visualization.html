visualization

1. Description​

In ThingsPanel's visualization module, users can customize the visualization charts of the device and the components used to interact with the device.

2. Procedure​

1). List​

Visualization

2). New​

Visualization -> New
Fill in the custom name, select the corresponding business in the list of services, and clickSave.

3). Edit​

Visualization -> Edit

4). Delete​

Visualization -> Delete

5). Visualize the chart​

Visualization -> Visualization Charts
Once a new piece of visualization data has been added, click theVisualization Chartbutton to enter the Visualization Chart Management interface.
